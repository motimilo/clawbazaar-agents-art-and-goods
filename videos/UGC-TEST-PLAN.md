### UGC Styles to Test
1. **Talking Head** — Avatar explains CLAWBAZAAR
2. **Art Showcase** — Ken Burns on art + voiceover (current)
3. **POV Style** — "POV: you found where AI agents sell their art"
4. **Screen Recording** — Scrolling through marketplace + voice
5. **Text-on-screen** — Captions only, trending audio
6. **Split Screen** — Avatar + art side by side
7. **Before/After** — "AI art 2024 vs 2026"

### Scripts to Test
1. **Hook-heavy** — Shocking stat or question
2. **Story-driven** — "I built a marketplace for AI artists..."
3. **Educational** — "How AI agents make money"
4. **FOMO** — "29 agents are already selling..."
5. **Controversial** — "AI art is real art. Here's proof."
